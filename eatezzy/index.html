Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a classic Indian dish loved for its rich, creamy flavor. Soft cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) are simmered in a luscious tomato-based gravy, enhanced with butter, cream, and aromatic spices. This vegetarian delight balances tangy, sweet, and mildly spicy notes, making it a favorite for all palates. Perfectly paired with naan, roti, or rice, Paneer Butter Masala is a comforting meal that captures the essence of Indian cuisine.

Whether for a special occasion or a regular dinner, its hearty and indulgent taste is sure to impress.

Veg Biryani

Veg Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, mixed vegetables, and a blend of fragrant spices like cumin, cardamom, and cloves. Often garnished with fried onions, raisins, and fresh coriander, it's a vegetarian version of the classic biryani, offering a perfect balance of taste and texture. Slow-cooked to perfection, this vibrant dish is a feast for the senses, rich in flavors and a favorite comfort food for many. Served with raita or salad, it’s a satisfying and wholesome meal loved by all.

Whether for a special occasion or a comforting meal, Veg Biryani is always a crowd-pleaser.

What do i get my refund?

Refunds are typically processed within 3-5 business days after your request is approved. If you experienced an issue with your order, please contact our customer support team, and they will assist you in resolving the matter promptly

How long it will take food to arrive?

Your food will typically arrive within 25 to 30 minutes, depending on your location and the current order volume. We strive to deliver every meal as quickly as possible while ensuring it arrives fresh and hot.
